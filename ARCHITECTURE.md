## PART 2 — CONTENT CLUSTER STRATEGY

### The Cluster Model

Each cluster has three layers:
```
HUB PAGE          — overview, links to all articles
  └── ARTICLES    — individual topic pages (target: 8–12 per cluster)
        └── LINKS — out to condition page + service page + location page + contact
```

Google reads a complete cluster as topical authority.
A half-built cluster provides little to no SEO benefit.
**Complete one cluster before starting the next.**

## PART 3 — ARTICLE TEMPLATE (use for every article)

Every article must follow this structure to maximize SEO and conversions:

```
1. TITLE           H1 — includes primary keyword naturally
2. INTRO           2–3 sentences — empathy first, then what they'll learn
3. EYEBROW         "For families navigating [topic] in Toronto"
4. BODY            700–1,200 words — H2 subheadings every 200–300 words
5. MID-CTA         After 3rd section — phone number or inline consultation form
6. FAQ SECTION     3–5 questions (triggers FAQPage schema in Google)
7. RELATED LINKS   "You may also find helpful:" — 2 articles + 1 condition page
8. BOTTOM CTABLOCK Full CTABlock component — phone + form
```

**Required in every article:**
- Primary keyword in: title, first 100 words, one H2, meta description
- Toronto / GTA reference at least twice
- Arcadia mentioned naturally 2–3 times (not stuffed)
- Internal links: 1 condition page, 1 service page, 1 location page, 2 articles
- Schema: Article + BreadcrumbList + FAQPage
