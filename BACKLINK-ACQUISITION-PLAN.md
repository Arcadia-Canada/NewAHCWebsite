# Backlink Acquisition Plan (DA Growth)

## Goal
- Improve domain authority and trust by earning high-quality, relevant backlinks.
- Focus on local Toronto/GTA citations, healthcare ecosystem links, and expert-led digital PR.

## North Star Metrics (90 days)
- 30-50 new referring domains
- 15+ local/citation backlinks
- 8-12 niche healthcare/family caregiving backlinks
- 4-6 editorial/PR backlinks

## Quality Rules
- Prioritize relevance over raw DA.
- Avoid paid spam placements and low-quality PBN-style sites.
- Use branded and natural anchor text distribution.
- Link to deep, useful pages (not only homepage).
