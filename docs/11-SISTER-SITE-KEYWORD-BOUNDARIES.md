# 11 — Sister-Site Keyword Boundaries (arcadiarehab.ca)

Cross-domain cannibalization rules between **arcadiahomecare.ca** (this site) and **arcadiarehab.ca** (sister site).

**Canonical version:** maintained in the AHC-Rehab repo as `arcadia-sister-site-keyword-boundaries.md`. When boundaries change, update **both files** and bump the "Last reviewed" date.

Last reviewed: June 2026

---

## Golden rule

| User intent | Owner |
|---|---|
| **DO something** — exercise, train, prevent falls, strengthen | **Arcadia Rehab** |
| **HELP WITH something** — bathe, dress, supervise, condition management | **Arcadia Home Care** (this site) |
| **Understand a condition** | **Home Care condition page** — Rehab may write exercise content and link here |

---

## This site owns (Rehab must not compete)

See full table in canonical file. Key territories:

- All **service pages** (`/our-services/*`) — PSW, dementia care, hospital discharge, **RSW rehabilitation support**, etc.
- All **condition pages** (`/conditions/*`)
- All **location pages** (`/locations/*`) — "home care [city]"
- **Resource articles** on dementia signs, home care decisions, caregiver burnout, costs, PSW roles
- Keywords in `10-KEYWORD-OWNERSHIP.md`

**Before publishing a new HC article**, check whether Rehab already targets the same primary keyword in the competitor guide or SEO architecture.

---

## Rehab owns (this site must not compete)

- Senior exercise how-tos (balance, strength, chair exercises, IADL-mapped workouts)
- Aquatherapy for seniors (`arcadiarehab.ca/aquatherapy/`)
- Fall prevention **exercise** programs (HC owns frailty/supervision angle on `/conditions/frailty-fall-prevention/`)
- RST entity and `/what-is-an-rst/`
- Proactive independence content (`/independence/`, IADL assessment)
- `senior rehabilitation Toronto` geo (Rehab `/toronto/` — not "home care Toronto")

**Do not publish on Home Care:**

- Duplicate exercise tutorial articles that mirror Rehab blog posts
- A competing "normal aging vs dementia" article if Rehab ever republishes (currently deferred to HC)
- Content targeting "rehabilitation support therapist" or RST keywords

## Naming clarity: RSW vs RST

| | Home Care | Arcadia Rehab |
|---|---|---|
| Role | **RSW** — Rehabilitation Support Worker | **RST** — Rehabilitation Support Therapist |
| Service page | `/our-services/rehabilitation-support/` | `/what-is-an-rst/` |
| Model | Clinician-supervised programs; ABI/stroke/complex recovery; insurance paths | Proactive private-pay exercise for elderly independence |

When writing about "rehabilitation support" on **this site**, mean **RSW**. Do not describe the RST service — link to arcadiarehab.ca instead.

---

## Cross-linking to Rehab

Link to Rehab when:

- Family may benefit from **proactive exercise** before home care is needed
- Content discusses strength, balance, fall prevention exercises, or cognitive exercise
- Triage article identifies RST-level need vs PSW-level need

Example framing: *"If your parent can still exercise but needs guidance staying strong, Arcadia Rehab provides in-home programs through Rehabilitation Support Therapists."*
